Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my Target Persona?

When you first open the app, our AI coach will guide you through a conversation to define who you want to become. Just answer honestly and the AI will help craft your personalized persona.

Can I change my persona or actions later?

Yes! You can edit your benchmarks and daily actions anytime. Premium users can also add new benchmarks and create additional personas.

What's the difference between Free and Premium?

Free users get 1 persona, 3 AI reflections per month, and core tracking features. Premium unlocks unlimited personas, unlimited AI coaching, and custom benchmarks.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your device's App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android) settings.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes. All your personal data is stored locally on your device. AI conversations use encrypted connections and are not used to train AI models. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What is the 120 Second Kickstart?

Each action has a quick 2-minute version to help build momentum on difficult days. It's designed to maintain your streak while respecting your energy levels.
