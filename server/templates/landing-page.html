Tools That Drive Real Change

Each feature solves a specific problem that keeps people stuck. Here's how they help you succeed.

Target Persona

Problem: Vague goals like "get healthier" lack direction and fade quickly.

Solution: Define a clear identity - "I am a disciplined athlete" - that guides every decision naturally.

Momentum Calendar

Problem: Missing one day makes you feel like a failure, destroying motivation.

Solution: Visual momentum tracking shows overall progress, not just streaks. One missed day doesn't erase your growth.

AI Coaching

Problem: You get stuck in the same patterns with no one to provide fresh perspective.

Solution: Monthly AI check-ins analyze your progress, spot patterns you miss, and adapt your strategy.

Progress Tracking

Problem: Without feedback, you don't know if you're actually improving.

Solution: Watch your persona alignment grow over time. See exactly how daily actions compound into real transformation.

Core Benchmarks

Problem: Big goals feel overwhelming with no clear path forward.

Solution: Break your persona into 3-5 key life areas, each with specific daily actions that make progress tangible.

120-Second Kickstart

Problem: Low energy days lead to skipping actions, breaking momentum.

Solution: Every action has a 2-minute version. Just show up. Consistency beats intensity.
