# Google Play listing copy

## App name

Resolution Companion AI

## Short description

Become who you're becoming with daily actions, milestones, and an AI coach.

## Full description

Resolution Companion is an identity-based companion for meaningful change.
Instead of chasing a number or protecting a fragile streak, define the person
you are becoming and reinforce that identity through small daily actions.

CREATE YOUR TARGET PERSONA

Have a focused conversation with the AI guide to describe the identity you
want to build. Resolution Companion turns that vision into practical
milestones and daily actions you can edit at any time.

MAKE TODAY COUNT

See the actions scheduled for today, use a smaller kickstart version when life
gets difficult, and record progress with one tap. Missing a day never erases
the work you have already done.

SEE THE JOURNEY

Review monthly consistency, momentum, completed milestones, recaps, and the
evidence that your actions are aligning with your chosen identity.

REFLECT WITH AN AI COACH

Use guided check-ins to notice wins, learn from lapses, and decide what matters
next. The coach is motivational and informational; it is not therapy, medical
care, or a substitute for professional advice. Any AI response can be reported
in the app for safety review.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

Your personas, milestones, actions, and progress history stay in local storage
on your device. AI messages are sent for processing only when you choose to use
an AI feature. Resolution Companion has no ads and does not track you across
apps or websites.

The free experience includes one active persona, daily action tracking,
Journey progress, and a monthly allowance of Coach check-ins. Premium adds
additional personas, milestones, and expanded coaching access through monthly
or yearly subscriptions. Prices and renewal terms are shown by Google Play
before purchase. Cancel anytime in Google Play; access continues through the
paid period.

Privacy: https://resolutioncompanion.com/privacy

## Console declarations draft

- App or game: App
- Category: Productivity
- Free or paid: Free, with in-app subscriptions
- Ads: No
- App access: All core app functionality is available without login; license
  testers use Google Play test purchases for Premium review
- Target audience: not designed for children; select the adult/general age
  brackets supported by the final content-rating answers
- Generative AI: yes; text-to-text onboarding and coaching; every generated
  response has an in-app Report action
- Data deletion: Profile > Privacy & Data > Delete My Account & Data deletes
  local data and the anonymous server device/subscription record

### Data Safety inputs to verify in Play Console

- Device or other identifiers: collected for app functionality, subscription
  validation, abuse limits, and deletion; not linked to a named identity; not
  used for advertising or tracking
- App interactions: aggregate daily event counts for analytics/app
  functionality; no advertising or tracking
- User-generated content: AI prompts are transmitted to OpenAI for app
  functionality and are not stored by Resolution Companion; when a user
  explicitly reports a generated response, the response is stored for safety
  review
- Purchase information: subscription product, state, token-derived provider
  reference, and expiration are stored for app functionality and fraud
  prevention; payment details remain with Google
- Encryption in transit: Yes
- Independent security review: do not claim unless completed
- Account creation: No
